THE ARTIST

Made by hand, held by wonder

Kindness Grace is a self-taught sculptor whose work spans the world of living things — from the ocean floor to ancient forests, from real creatures to mythic beasts. Every piece begins with paper and a vision, and ends as something that insists on being noticed.

Working primarily in paper mache and acrylic paint, Kindness Grace builds dimension, texture, and life from the most humble of materials. No two pieces are alike. Each one is signed, one-of-a-kind, and made to last.

To inquire about a piece, commission a custom work, or ask about shipping, reach out directly. Pieces sell quickly — the creatures don't wait long.
