Our Story

We are not selling furniture.
We are selling emotional time travel.

Finders Keepers Market was born from a love of objects that carry memory. A 1959 Zenith TV reimagined as a fish tank. A 1960s console reimagined as a fully stocked bar — complete with the original Breuner's receipt still in the drawer.

We are a Bay Area family of makers, finders, and artists. Every piece is one of a kind. Every piece has a story. Come find something worth keeping.
